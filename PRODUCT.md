# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Internal eTranzact SREs, IT Operations, Support Teams, and Admins monitoring, triaging, and analyzing downtime, security incidents, and fraud.

## Product Purpose
Unified incident management, SLA compliance tracking, and knowledge base search to minimize service disruptions and resolve critical incidents quickly.

## Brand Personality
Professional, precise, high-fidelity.

## Anti-references
Overly spaced SaaS layouts, mesh gradients, neon AI-style purple glows, Bento grids for non-complex lists, and em dashes.

## Design Principles
1. Information Density over Whitespace: Content must be highly scannable; data-rich layouts should use compact, well-proportioned spacing.
2. Color as Information, Not Decoration: Color indicates severity (Critical, High, Medium, Low) and incident domain (Downtime, Security, Fraud). Avoid decorative accents that conflict with this taxonomy.
3. Dark Mode First Class: Ensure absolute parity and visual balance between light and dark themes.
4. Colorblind Accessibility: Status and severity levels must combine distinct visual indicators (icons/text) with high-contrast color.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance for contrast ratios. Support colorblind users via visual symbols and text alongside color coding.
